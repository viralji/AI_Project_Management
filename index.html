Core AI Concepts for PMs

üß† LLM (Large Language Model)

Neural networks trained on the entire internet to understand and generate human language.

üìù Context Window

AI's "working memory". Bigger window = AI can read entire books or project histories at once.

üéØ Prompt Engineering

The skill of asking AI the right questions. Better prompts = 10x better results.

üé® Multimodal AI

AI that can see (images), hear (audio), and speak, not just read text.

üîç RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation)

AI that pulls from your project documents to give accurate, context-specific answers instead of generic responses.

üéØ Fine-tuning

Training AI on your specific project data to make it an expert in your domain and terminology.

üî¢ Token

The basic unit AI processes. ~750 words = 1000 tokens. Understanding this helps manage AI costs and limits.

üìä Embeddings

AI converts text into numbers that capture meaning. Enables semantic search across project documents.

ü§ñ AI Agent

Autonomous AI that can plan, use tools, and execute tasks end-to-end without constant human input.

‚ö†Ô∏è Hallucination

When AI confidently generates false information. Critical to verify AI outputs, especially for project decisions.

üîó Chain of Thought

AI reasoning step-by-step, showing its logic. Essential for complex project problem-solving and transparency.

üìö Few-shot Learning

Teaching AI by showing just a few examples. Perfect for customizing AI to your project templates and formats.
